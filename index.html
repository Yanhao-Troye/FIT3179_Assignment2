Worldwide Unicorn Companies

A unicorn means a privately owned startup valued at over $1 billion. The term "unicorn" has been widely used to refer to startups in technology, mobile technology, and information technology. Now, I'm going to show you some information about unicorns and hopefully provide you some insight into future investments and business.

Unicorn Companies all over the world

As of March 30, 2022, there were over 1000 unicorns in the world. More than half of the unicorns are in the United States. Another 180 unicorns are in China. India ranks third in the international Unicorn rankings, followed by the United Kingdom and Germany. Unicorns are now found on every continent, but a glance at the map shows that their populations are relatively small in Latin America and Africa.

Top 5 countries with most unicorns and their industry flow

By the distribution of unicorn industries across major countries, you can determine whether there are opportunities based on the characteristics of the region. As we can see USA and China dominate in terms of the diversity of industries. Most U.S. unicorns are concentrated in Internet Software and Fintech. Meanwhile, China's unicorns are more evenly distributed across various sectors. Other countries, like India and the UK, show industry specialization but at a smaller scale.

Top 10 valuable companies

Now, you may be wonder which unicorns in the world are relatively successful, and among the top 10 largest unicorns in the world, Chinese artificial intelligence company Bytedance is the largest, with a valuation of $140 billion, followed by Elon Musk's Space X and Chinese fashion retailer Shein. The top 10 also included Australian graphic design platform Canva and British payment service Checkout.com etc.

Unicorn company industry valuation

It is also important to understand the valuation of each industry, with higher estimates indicating better prospects for growth, but also facing greater competition.Some other industries, although the valuation of unicorn companies is less, but there may be more opportunities, of course, this is not absolute, we have to consider different industries according to the social situation and regional advantages.
